HTML Status Messages

We all have often came across Error message on the websites we surf like "404: Not Found" or "502: Bad Gateway". However, they are not error messages, they are known as HTML status messages. These messages are sent by the server even when the websites are working properly. It is just a check which takes place for proper functioning of the website.
